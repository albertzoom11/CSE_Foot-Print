Procedure

  1. Unbox product, which contains shoe insoles (two) of specified size and plastic boxes (two) that have wires. Each plastic box is wired to one sole and two velcro strips with adhesive on the back of the strips
  2. Open the back of both plastic boxes.
  3. Insert the CR2032 coin batteries into the backs of each plastic box.
  4. Close the backs of both plastic boxes.
  5. Adhere the velcro strips to the sides of each shoe, with one velcro strip to one shoe.
  6. Stick each plastic box to its designated velcro strip (the plastic box that has the sole for the right foot should attach itself to the velcro strip on the right shoe, and vice versa for the plastic box that has the sole for the left foot).
  7. Insert each insole into its respective shoe.
  8. Turn on the soles with the switches on the plastic boxes.
  9. Sign up on the website.
  10. Put the shoes on (with the soles in them).
  11. Engage in running.
  12. Observe the results on the app at the conclusion of the run: including a heat map and feedback from the app.
  13. Feedback from the app will tell whether one is putting too much pressure on the bridge, ball, pinky side, or heel of the foot. If there are no issues, the app will suggest no changes. This information will be reinforced in the heatmap provided in the app.
  14. From the feedback received from the website, adjust oneâ€™s running technique to take force off of the specified overstressed area. For example, if one is putting too much force on the ball or pinky side, then try to strike with the heel of the foot more.
